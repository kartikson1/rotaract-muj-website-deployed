Who are we?

Rotaract Green Club

The Rotaract Green club is a non profit organization at Manipal University Jaipur. The club aims at inculcating awareness and incubating an environment of social service in the university. It is one of the most prestigious and respected organizations of MUJ. RGC has an active presence in the social sector and has been organizing social welfare related activities and programs in the University.

Departments

The various Departments in the club are Human Resources, Design, Social Media, Media, Editorial, Event, Promotions, Gram Vikas.

Villages

The various villages under Rotaract Green Club MUJ are :

  • Dehmi Kalan
  • Dehmi Khurd
  • Sanjharia
  • Thekariya
  • Begas

Questions and Answers

Why should we join this club? How does it help in development?

Rotaract provides an opportunity to enhance knowledge and skills that will assist them in personal development in all the ways.

What are the responsibilities of it's members ?

The members are expected to attend club meetings and participate in club activities or projects.

If I'm not a Rotaract Club member, can I still help out with events & projects ?

Yes, you can still help out with events & projects even if you're not a Rotaract Club member.
