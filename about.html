  • About us

    The Rotaract Green club is a non profit organization at Manipal University Jaipur . The club is aimed at inculcating awareness and incubating an environment of social service in the university. It is one of the most prestigious and respected organizations of MUJ. RGC has an active presence in the social sector and has been organizing social welfare related activities and programs in the University.

WHO WE ARE


The Rotaract Club of MUJ is a youth social working society formed in order to serve the needy people and nature. The overall outcome of the club is beneficial for both the members and the nation.
Rotaract provides an opportunity to enhance knowledge and skills that will assist them in personal development, to address the physical and social needs of their communities, and promote better relations through framework of friendship and service.
