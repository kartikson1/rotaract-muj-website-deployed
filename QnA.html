Questions and Answers

What is a Rotaract club ?

The Rotaract Green Club of MUJ is a youth social working society formed in order to serve our nature and the needy people . The overall outcome of the club is beneficial for both the members and the nation. We believe to take action through service.

What responsibilities befall on the club's members ?

The members are expected to attend club meetings and participate in club activities/ projects. They are also expected to contribute ideas about any social events that can be organised. Clubs encourage to aspire to club leadership roles.

Why should we join this club?

Rotaract provides an opportunity to enhance knowledge and skills that will assist them in personal development, to address the physical and social needs of their communities, and promote better relations through framework of friendship and service.

What are the activities and projects that we do ?

From social awareness, technical solutions, visits to villages allocated under our club district, to blood donation programme, to more evident cleaning drives and plantation, the club has been working extensively on such projects and pioneers path breaking ideas for the future.

If I'm not a Rotaract Club member, can I still help out with events & projects ?

Yes, you can still help out with events & projects even if you're not a Rotaract Club member.

Is it good for your resume?

Having it on your resume is an added plus in the volunteer experience section as it is a service club. The major avenues of Rotaract are Professional & Personal development and Community service. With 1.2 million Rotarians around the world chances are that person interviewing you is one of them.
